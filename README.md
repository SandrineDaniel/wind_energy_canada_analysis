# Wind Energy and Sustainable Energy Projects

This repository contains academic reports written during my Erasmus exchange at **Linnaeus University (Sweden)** for the course **Sustainable Energy Supply**.

The reports focus on renewable energy systems, particularly wind power and its role in the energy transition.

---

## Context

These projects were completed as part of my studies abroad during my Erasmus semester in Sweden.

The goal of the course was to analyze renewable energy technologies, evaluate their feasibility, and understand their role in sustainable energy systems.

---

## Reports

### Wind Energy in Canada

This report analyzes the development of wind energy in Canada and its contribution to the national electricity system.

Topics covered include:

- history of wind energy development in Canada
- current installed wind capacity
- regional distribution of wind farms
- future potential of wind power in the Canadian energy mix

The report also discusses the role of wind energy in Canada's **net-zero emissions target by 2050**.

---

### Feasibility of a 60 MW Wind Farm in Alberta

This report evaluates the feasibility of building a **60 MW wind power plant in southern Alberta, Canada**.

The analysis includes:

- site selection and wind resource potential
- estimated electricity production
- project investment costs
- expected revenues
- financial feasibility analysis

The project estimates annual production of approximately **150 GWh of electricity** and evaluates the economic viability of the installation.
